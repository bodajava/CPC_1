AUTOMOTIVE OILS

Made for smooth engine performance. Reduce friction, prevent deposits, and extend engine life. Available in grades 20W‑50, 10W‑40 … (API SJ–SN).

DIESEL ENGINE OIL

Meets API CF / CI‑4 / CJ‑4; handles high‑temperature heavy‑duty use. Cuts sludge & soot. Viscosities 15W‑40, 10W‑30.

MOTORCYCLE & OUTBOARD

2T low‑smoke oils for scooters, 4T high‑performance for big bikes. Outboard TC‑W3 oils for marine engines.

TRANSMISSION OILS

ATF & gear oils protect metal, cool gearboxes, prevent wear. Grades 75W‑90, 80W‑90 … for smooth shifting & longer life.

BRAKE FLUID

DOT‑3 / DOT‑4 fluids with high boiling point to avoid brake failure – engineered for performance & safety.
