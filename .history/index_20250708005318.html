CPC Oils

CPC OilS are produced under efficient programed control system in the chemical labs which are equipped by the latest equipment.

If any more detailed information or clarification is required on the products listed or not covered, CO-OP marketing & Sales division will be happy to provide this to any customer and follow up with free technical assistance where necessary.

AUTOMOTIVE OILS

Made for smooth engine performance. Reduce friction, prevent deposits, and extend engine life. Available in different grades like 20W-50, 10W-40, etc. Certified with API ratings like SJ, SL, SM, SN

DEISEL ENGINES OIL

Meet API CF, CI-4, CJ-4 standards. Withstand high-temperature operations and heavy-duty use. Reduce sludge and soot buildup in diesel engines. Available in viscosities like 15W-40, 10W-30.

MOTORCYCLE & OUTBOARD

2T (2-stroke) oils: For scooters and small engines, with low smoke. 4T (4-stroke) oils: High-performance oils for larger motorcycles. Outboard oils are often TC-W3 rated, designed for marine use.

TRANSMISSIONS OILS

CPC offers ATF (Automatic Transmission Fluid) and gear oils. Protects metal surfaces, cools the gearbox, and prevents wear. Viscosity grades: 75W-90, 80W-90, etc. Helps ensure smooth gear shifting and long transmission life.

BRAKE FLUID

CPC produces DOT-3 and DOT-4 brake fluids. High boiling point to avoid brake failure. Designed for both performance and safety in extreme driving conditions.
