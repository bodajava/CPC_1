AUTOMOTIVE OILS

Made for smooth engine performance. Reduce friction, prevent deposits, and extend engine life. Available in grades 20W‑50, 10W‑40 … (API SJ–SN).

DIESEL ENGINE OIL

Meets API CF / CI‑4 / CJ‑4; handles high‑temperature heavy‑duty use. Cuts sludge & soot. Viscosities 15W‑40, 10W‑30.

MOTORCYCLE & OUTBOARD

TRANSMISSION OILS

BRAKE FLUID

Let our experience be the answer to all your industrial lubricants questions

Description: Heavy‑duty industrial gear oil with Flender‑approved additives that prevent micro‑pitting on gear teeth.

Usage: For spur, helical, bevel gears under heavy/shock loads, slow‑speed bearings, and wind‑turbine gearboxes.
